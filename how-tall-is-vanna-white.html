Who is Vanna White?

Vanna White Wikipedia: - Vanna Marie Rosich was born on February 18, 1957, in Horry County, South Carolina. She is a popular television personality and game-show hostess from the United States. Since 1982, she has served as co-host of the game program Wheel of Fortune. In 1978, while pursuing a career in fashion, she started modeling and participated in Miss Georgia USA. She made her debut on a game show on The Price Is Right on June 20, 1980, where she was one of the first four competitors.

She has featured as herself or in supporting roles in numerous movies and television shows in addition to her work on Wheel of Fortune. She is also the author of the 1987 autobiography Vanna Speaks. She has had cameo appearances in movies like Double Dragon and the Australian variety show Hey Hey It's Saturday in addition to television shows including L.A. Law, 227, Super Mario Bros. Super Show, Simon and Simon, The King of Queens, Full House, and RuPaul's Drag Race All Stars. She received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame on April 20, 2006.
