Born in Kinshasa, DRC, Mutombo moved to the United States to attend Georgetown University on a USAID scholarship. He initially intended to become a doctor but later shifted his focus to basketball. His towering height and natural talent quickly made him a standout player.

Mutombo’s professional career began when he was drafted by the Denver Nuggets in 1991. He played for several NBA teams, including the Atlanta Hawks, Philadelphia 76ers, New Jersey Nets, and Houston Rockets. His defensive prowess earned him the NBA Defensive Player of the Year award four times.
