Scott Disick Steps Out Looking Almost Unrecognizable After Skipping Yeezy Season 3 Show

The Lord is looking a little different these days. Scott Disick stepped out in NYC on Friday, February 12, to party with Tyga, Kylie Jenner and Rihanna, and the troubled reality star looked a bit worse for the wear. 

Disick, 32, wore a plain black T-shirt, jeans and tan-colored kicks for the outing to New York hotspot Up & Down following his previously advertised appearance at 1 Oak.

“Friday night I’m hosting @1oaknyc #theinstatution for a very special fashion week celebration with my brother @kinggoldchains and @richie_akiva #bestisyettocome,” he captioned a promotional poster of himself and Tyga, 26.

According to TMZ, Disick was spotted sipping on beers despite having left rehab just three months ago.

On Thursday, Disick was notably absent from pal Kanye West’s much-anticipated Yeezy Season 3 fashion show at Madison Square Garden, though the entire Kardashian-Jenner clan attended (even Khloé Kardashian’s estranged husband, Lamar Odom, made an appearance).

But a source tells Us Weekly that Disick’s failure to show up was not a diss.

“Scott was invited to Kanye’s show but there’s no drama behind him not attending,” the source says.

Late last month, Kourtney Kardashian sat down with Ellen DeGeneres to clarify her current relationship with Disick.

“We’re not together,” the mother of three said. “I feel like every day is different, but we’re totally getting along. Like, we have three beautiful kids together … He’s family. He’ll always be.”

Kourtney, 36, and Disick are parents to Mason, 6, Penelope, 3, and Reign, 13 months.
