Peter Billingsley and Buffy Bains: Engaged!! Still not married even after two years of Engagement! W

Peter Billingsley is an American actor who was born on the 16th of April, 1971 and is currently 51 years of age. The news of Peter and his girlfriend Buffy Bains getting engaged has taken the headlines.

About the engagement

Peter Billingsley is engaged to his girlfriend, Buffy Bains, his rep confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

The actor Peter, 51 is best known for playing Ralphie in the 1983 classic A Christmas Story. Back in 2010, his bride-to-be opened up about her life in Chicago for a video posted on the Chicago Young Republican.

“I love the big city feel, even though it’s still manageable,” the Colorado native said at the time. “It’s still easy to meet people and get involved with things you want to get involved in.”

Bains also noted that she graduated from Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas, and loves sailing.

Billingsley, meanwhile, has continued to work in Hollywood since his iconic role in the holiday fan favorite. In recent years, he appeared in Elf, Iron Man, and The Break-Up, which stars his best friend Vince Vaughn.

Are They Still Together?

The duo has been engaged for almost one and half years and still haven’t announced their wedding. There has been no news about them since they got engaged. We are not sure at the moment if the couple is together or not.

There was the time they were together and in love which led them to get engaged in 2015 but even after the engagement which has already been almost 2 years haven’t yet gotten married makes it suspicious for us about their relationship.

Though Peter currently works as a Hollywood producer, director,  and actor, he hasn’t hung up his black-framed glasses and bunny suit for good! Fans got to relive the holiday classic in 2010 when he brought his childhood role onto the stage for A Christmas Story: The Musical!.

At the time he revealed,

“The film brings a lot of joy to a lot of people,”

Adding,

“I always get asked, ‘Aren’t you over it?’ But I’m not.”

The former actor even kept some of the memorabilia from the set.

“I still have the gun, and I sadly still have that bunny suit.”

We do not much information about the duo since they haven’t been in the limelight for a long time and have given any hint since the news of them being engaged. They have been successful in keeping their personal life private. We as a fan are wondering about the relationship.

Additionally, we hope that they are still together and going strong since we have limited information. We are happy to see them as a couple and hope they get married soon!
